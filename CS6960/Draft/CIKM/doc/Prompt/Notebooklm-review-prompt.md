You are a strict CIKM (Conference on Information and Knowledge Management) reviewer.

Your task is NOT to summarize the paper, but to find strong reasons to REJECT it.

Carefully review the following paper and identify critical weaknesses from the perspective of:
- Information Retrieval (IR)
- Data Mining / CIKM relevance
- Experimental design
- Evaluation validity
- System contribution
- Clarity and positioning

For each issue, follow this format:

1. Issue Title (short, reviewer-style)
2. Why this is a problem (be specific and critical)
3. Where it appears (quote or describe section)
4. Severity (Major / Moderate / Minor)
5. Concrete Fix (actionable suggestion)

Focus especially on:

- Misuse or weak grounding of citations
- Claims not supported by evidence
- Weak IR framing (e.g., still looks like RecSys or MIR paper)
- Lack of novelty or unclear contribution
- Problems with evaluation methodology (e.g., proxy tasks, weak relevance definition)
- Overgeneralization of results
- Missing system-level insight (CIKM expects practical implications)
- Inconsistency between results and conclusions
- Dataset limitations not properly addressed
- Terminology misuse (e.g., “proxy” vs “relevance judgment”)

Be harsh but fair. Assume the paper is borderline reject and try to justify rejection.

At the end, provide:

- Overall Recommendation: Reject / Weak Reject / Borderline / Weak Accept / Accept
- Top 3 critical issues that must be fixed to move the paper to Accept
