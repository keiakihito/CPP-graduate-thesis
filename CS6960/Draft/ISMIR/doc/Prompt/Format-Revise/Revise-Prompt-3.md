Review the manuscript and identify paragraph opening sentences that are weak or not functioning well as topic sentences.

For each paragraph:

* Evaluate whether the first sentence clearly communicates the main idea of the paragraph
* Identify paragraphs where the opening is vague, too general, or delayed

For each issue:

* Quote the original first sentence
* Explain briefly why it is weak or suboptimal
* Suggest a stronger alternative opening sentence

IMPORTANT:

* Do NOT rewrite the entire paragraph
* Do NOT change the argument or claims
* Only propose improved first sentences

Output format:

* Section / paragraph location
* Original sentence
* Suggested improved sentence
* Short explanation
