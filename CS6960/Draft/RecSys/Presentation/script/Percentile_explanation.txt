⏺ Here's the verbal explanation you can use during the presentation when you're on the Label           
  Construction slide:                                                                                  
                                                                                                       
  ---                                                                                                  
  "We started with a simple, principled choice: split the VA space into even thirds using the 33rd and
  67th percentiles. No tuning, no cherry-picking.                                                      
                                                 
  But we wanted to verify this wasn't arbitrary. When we plotted the actual VA scores for our 203      
  tracks, the scatter showed the dataset clusters tightly in a narrow mid-range band — roughly 0.30 to 
  0.50 on both axes. That's classical music: emotionally restrained, rarely extreme.
                                                                                                       
  What this confirmed is that our tertile boundaries land right at the edges of that dense region — not
   cutting through the middle of it. So Energetic captures the high-arousal tail, Calm the low-arousal
  tail, and Tense the low-valence tail.                                                                
                                                                                                       
  Lyrical is the special case — it targets the cross-section of mid-valence and mid-arousal, the 40th
  to 60th percentile band on both axes. That's the dense center cluster that none of the other three   
  labels would claim. Without it, the most common VA profile in our dataset would go unlabeled.
                                                                                                       
  So the percentile thresholds aren't arbitrary — the scatter plot validated that they carve the space
  where the actual data lives."                                                                        
                                                            
  ---                                                                                                  
  This gives you a clean 3-part arc: principled starting point → visual validation → Lyrical as the
  intentional center. You can trim it to 30 seconds or expand depending on audience questions.
